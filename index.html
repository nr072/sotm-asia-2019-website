Venue

Krishibid Institution Bangladesh (KIB)

KIBC Auditorium, the second Largest auditorium in Bangladesh, is owned and managed by Krishibid Institution Bangladesh (KIB). Established with a goal to facilitate professionals from the agricultural sector, this is now a place for knowledge development for all the members of the community.

State of the Map Asia 2018

Last year, we were in Bangalore, India. Members of the OpenStreetMap community met to learn the significance of mapping from the perspective of the different countries in Asia – from Afghanistan to Vietnam. 300+ participants from 12 countries, where one in every six participants was a speaker congregated at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore.
