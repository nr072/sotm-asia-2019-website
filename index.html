Last year, we were in Bangalore, India. Members of the OpenStreetMap community met to learn the significance of mapping from the perspective of the different countries in Asia – from Afghanistan to Vietnam. 300+ participants from 12 countries, where one in every six participants was a speaker congregated at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore.
