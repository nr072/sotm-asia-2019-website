Program

State of the Map Asia 2019 features two full days of talks, workshops, an opening and closing keynote. We have an exciting two days! The program is created by curating proposals from you - the community and through a scoring process by the committee.

The conference will be at BSS/NILG, Dhaka. Aside from that, we will have plenty of space set aside for breakout sessions and birds of a feather meet-ups. (Click here to download the program.)

Our friends from Janastu is organising a post conference gathering and hackathon in the Devarayanadurga hills and the IruWay farm. Find more details here and if you are interested send an e-mail to iruway@janastu.org.
